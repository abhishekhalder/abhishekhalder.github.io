Sensitivity Analysis using Neural Network for Estimating Aircraft Stability and Control Derivatives

R. Garhwal, A. Halder and M. Sinha

IEEE International Conference on Intelligent and Advanced Systems, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, Nov. 2007.

  • Paper

  • IEEE Xplore

Abstract: This paper presents a method for aircraft parameter estimation using neural sensitivity analysis. The results are found to be superior to other ANN based methods.
